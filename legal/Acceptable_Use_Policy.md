# Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

**Last Updated:** July 2026
**Company:** DagangOS Digital Indonesia

This AUP governs the use of software, APIs, and platforms provided by DagangOS Digital Indonesia. Violation of this policy may result in immediate suspension or termination of your software license and support agreements.

## Illegal Content
You may not use our software to publish, transmit, or facilitate any content that is illegal under Indonesian law or the laws of the jurisdiction in which your business operates. This includes, but is not limited to, the sale of illegal drugs, unverified pharmaceuticals, or contraband.

## Malware & Destructive Code
You may not use the Platform to distribute viruses, worms, Trojan horses, ransomware, or any other malicious code intended to disrupt or damage networks, hardware, or data.

## Spam & Unsolicited Communications
The Platform (including its CRM and mailing modules) may not be used to send unsolicited bulk email (Spam), SMS, or push notifications. All marketing communications must comply with anti-spam regulations and include clear opt-out mechanisms.

## Adult Content
Unless explicitly agreed upon in writing for specific industries, the standard DagangOS platform may not be used to host, distribute, or monetize explicit adult material, pornography, or non-consensual explicit content.

## Copyright & Intellectual Property
You must respect intellectual property rights. You may not use the Platform to distribute pirated software, counterfeit goods, or unauthorized copyrighted media.

## Reverse Engineering
While you own the data and your infrastructure, the underlying DagangOS modular framework remains proprietary. You may not reverse engineer, decompile, or attempt to extract the core source code of the Platform to build a competing product or resell the framework itself.

## API Abuse
Automated interactions with DagangOS APIs (including webhooks and developer endpoints) must be performed at reasonable rates. You may not subject the APIs to excessive request volumes that cause denial of service or degrade performance.

## DDoS & Network Attacks
You may not use the Platform or your infrastructure as a staging ground for Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attacks, network scanning, or unauthorized penetration testing of third-party networks.

## Cryptocurrency Mining
The Platform and any background worker processes provided by DagangOS may not be modified or hijacked to mine cryptocurrency.

## Server Abuse
While you own your VPS, you may not configure the DagangOS software in a manner that intentionally abuses shared network resources, exploits cloud provider loopholes, or violates the Terms of Service of your chosen infrastructure provider (e.g., AWS, DigitalOcean).
