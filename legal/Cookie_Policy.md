# Cookie Policy

**Last Updated:** July 2026
**Company:** DagangOS Digital Indonesia

This Cookie Policy explains how DagangOS Digital Indonesia uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our administrative dashboards, API portals, and marketing websites to recognize you, improve user experience, and ensure platform security.

## What are cookies?
Cookies are small data files placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used by online service providers to make websites work efficiently, securely manage user sessions, and provide reporting information.

## Essential Cookies
These cookies are strictly necessary to provide you with Services available through our Platform and to use some of its features.
* **Authentication:** Used to identify you once you log in (e.g., `next-auth.session-token`). Without these, you cannot access the admin dashboard or API portal.
* **Security:** Used to detect fraudulent activity and enforce CSRF (Cross-Site Request Forgery) protection (e.g., `next-auth.csrf-token`).
* **Tenant Routing:** Used to properly resolve multi-tenant architectures and map your session to the correct sub-domain or workspace.

## Analytics Cookies
These cookies collect information that is used in aggregate form to help us understand how our Platform is being used or how effective our marketing campaigns are.
* Examples include Google Analytics or self-hosted tracking solutions. 
* They track metrics such as page load times, error rates, and navigation paths, helping us optimize the UI/UX of the DagangOS ecosystem.

## Marketing Cookies
These cookies are used to make advertising messages more relevant to you on external websites. They perform functions like preventing the same ad from continuously reappearing and ensuring ads are properly displayed. 
*Note: Marketing cookies are generally only used on our public sales pages (`dagangos.com`), not within your private tenant admin dashboard.*

## Session vs. Persistent Cookies
* **Session Cookies:** Temporary cookies that expire and are automatically deleted when you close your browser. We use these for active login states.
* **Persistent Cookies:** Remain on your device for a set period or until manually deleted. We use these to remember preferences (like dark mode/light mode toggles or sidebar collapsed states).

## Third-Party Cookies
In some cases, we use cookies provided by trusted third parties, such as payment gateway iframes (e.g., Midtrans, Xendit, or Stripe) required to securely process subscription payments without routing credit card data through our servers.

## How can I control cookies?
You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject non-essential cookies. You can set or amend your web browser controls to accept or refuse cookies. If you choose to reject essential cookies (like authentication tokens), you will be unable to log into the DagangOS admin dashboard.

For more information on how to manage cookies, please visit the help pages of your respective browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge).
