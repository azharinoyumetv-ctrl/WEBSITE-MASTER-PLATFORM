# Refund Policy

**Last Updated:** July 2026
**Company:** PT DagangOS Digital Indonesia

At PT DagangOS Digital Indonesia, we strive to ensure absolute satisfaction with our B2B SaaS and custom development services. This policy outlines the conditions under which refunds may be issued.

## Eligible Refunds
Refunds are strictly evaluated on a case-by-case basis and are generally only eligible if DagangOS fails to deliver the agreed-upon Services as defined in the Statement of Work (SOW).

## Website Development & Software
Due to the custom nature of software and website development, **deposits are strictly non-refundable** once development work has commenced. If a project is cancelled by the Client prior to the commencement of coding/setup (e.g., during the initial design phase), a partial refund may be issued minus administrative and consulting fees.

## Subscriptions (SaaS Modules)
For ongoing monthly or annual module subscriptions (e.g., AI Copywriter, CRM add-ons):
* **Monthly:** We do not offer prorated refunds for monthly subscriptions. You may cancel at any time to prevent future billing.
* **Annual:** If you cancel an annual subscription within the first 14 days, you are eligible for a prorated refund minus a 1-month administrative fee.

## Hosting, Domain & VPS
Because DagangOS operates on a **client-owned infrastructure model**, we do not collect fees for domains, VPS, or database hosting. You pay your chosen provider (e.g., AWS, DigitalOcean, Cloudflare) directly. 
**Therefore, DagangOS cannot issue refunds for infrastructure costs.** You must consult your specific infrastructure provider for their refund policies.

## Maintenance Agreements
Pre-paid maintenance retainers are non-refundable. If you choose to terminate a maintenance agreement early, services will continue until the end of the current billing cycle.

## Custom Development
Payments made for custom module development, bespoke integrations, or "Custom Quote" enterprise tiers are non-refundable once the deliverables have passed the User Acceptance Testing (UAT) phase or if the delay/failure is caused by the Client's inability to provide necessary API keys, assets, or feedback.

## Cancellation Process
To initiate a cancellation or request a refund evaluation, you must submit a formal written request to `billing@dagangos.com` detailing your project ID and the reason for the request.

## Refund Timeline
If a refund is approved by our billing department, it will be processed and credited back to the original method of payment within 14-21 business days. Bank transfer timelines may vary based on your financial institution.
